Pat also thanks people unknown for so creatively interpreting the INCITS/ANSI T10/T13 SCSI standards:

  • the ANSI T10 & T13 committee members who share stories of confusion

  • the Microsoft programmer of SCSI hosts who first chose to discard the offset 4 AdditionalLength field of SPC op x12 Inquiry data

  • the Microsoft programmer of SCSI devices who first zeroed the offset 4 AdditionalLength field of SPC op x12 Inquiry data

  • the Connectix programmer of SCSI devices who first zeroed the offset 7 AdditionalLength field of SPC op x03 RequestSense data
