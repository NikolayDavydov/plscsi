Since October 31, 2002:

Not only do the six char disk/cd drive names "\\.\$letter:" work, for instance \\.\I:, but also the nine or more char names "\\.\TAPE$n", for instance \\.\TAPE0. However, we find the latter work only with tape devices that appear listed in DeviceManager as "Tape drives", rather than as "Unknown devices".
